Psychoshock

"Will you be our next test subject?

In the laboratory research is done on fear. For peculiar experiments they are continuously looking for fresh test subjects. We have warned you... Dare to enter Psychoshock.


Inside Psychoshock...
You only have to wait about 15 minutes to enter this asylum. Once inside, there are scary dentists, doctors and prisoners waiting for you. It smells like death and dentist. You hear the dental drill and the person screaming laying on the chair. The prisoners are trying to get you to join them and the doctor is laughing disturbingly. Try to make it outside and run as fast as you can...


My opinion
To be honest, I really hated this Haunted House because I hate dentists and doctors, but I had to laugh half of the time because my friends were screaming so loud. It was a nice Haunted House. You want to see how it was inside? Watch this video: Short Film
