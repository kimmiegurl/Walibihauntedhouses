The Villa

"Do you want to play the leading role in a horror film?"

Always wanted to play the leading role in a Hollywood film? Auditions aren't necessary during Halloween. Enter The Villa and get into a Hollywood hell... Are you sure you want to shine as a moviestar??


Inside The Villa...
This queue isn't as long as the others, you only have to wait for about 30 minutes. Before you can go inside a female is waiting for you, she just starts looking at you, without saying a word and finally after a competition of staring she will scare you. Inside The Villa it looks like a drama film, people screaming bloody murder, people coming for you, pretending to grab you or throw stuff at you. Someone going crazy while being tied down on a bed. And when you think you're safe outside of The Villa, Chucky will be there...
