Walibi Fright Nights Haunted Houses


"You'll wish it was a nightmare..."

The Walibi Fright Nights is an event which takes place once a year, from the 8th of October until the 30th of October. It is every Friday, Saturday and Sunday. The idea of The Fright Nights is actually to escape and conquer your fears and meanwhile you can go into the attractions, haunted houses and have fun. There are scarezones, four normal haunted houses and one extreme haunted house, that's called the Clinic.


Tickets
There are several kinds of tickets, which are only available online. There are group tickets, for groups between 10 and 19 people, the price per person is €30,-. There are tickets for bigger groups, for 20+ people, the price per person is €28,50. There are also individual ticktes, which are €37,50. Several arrangements are available, such as the R.I.P deal for €99,95, the bloody R.I.P. for €89,95 and the Haunted Deal for €59,95. Price per Haunted House is €5,50 and The Clinic is €15,- with a minimum age of 16 years for The Clinic. For further information visit the original site: Walibi Holland
